Our Belief

What We Stand For

This is a Church which believes in Trinity — The Father, The Son & The Holy Spirit God. The main objective & goal of this Church is to bring plenty of souls into the Kingdom of God.

This church preaches & believes in Heaven & Eternal Hell, the Birth, Crucifixion, Death, Resurrection & Second Coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, Sin & Repentance, Holiness, Salvation, Healing, Miracles, Blessings, Prophecy, Faith, Hope & Love.

As a Church, the body of Christ, we are expecting a great end-time Revival as in the days of the apostles — with the 9 gifts and fruits of the Spirit.

"And this gospel of the kingdom will be preached in all the world as a witness to all nations, and then the end will come."

Maranatha! 🙏 Lord Jesus Come Soon 🔜
